This page needs the optional InsForge backend. When web/insforge-config.js exists (copy web/insforge-config.example.js and fill in your project's URL + anon key), the form below will insert your submission into the hosted submissions table — a moderated queue the harness operator pulls from. Until then the form is disabled; nothing you type here is sent anywhere.

Submissions are stored, not executed: nothing runs automatically. An operator reviews the queue, runs the agent locally against picked entries, and publishes the resulting learning logs. Don't submit anything secret — the queue is a plain database table.
